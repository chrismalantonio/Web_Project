About TSM

Team SoloMid (abbreviated TSM) is an eSports organization based in the United States. It was founded in September 2009 by Andy "Reginald" Dinh. TSM currently fields players in League of Legends, Hearthstone, Super Smash Bros., PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds, Fortnite and Vainglory. The organization previously had a Counter-Strike: Global Offensive team based in Denmark and also briefly owned an Overwatch team. TSM's League of Legends team has won six of the total ten splits of the North American League of Legends Championship Series (LCS). It has also participated in every League of Legends World Championship.
